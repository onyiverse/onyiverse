Welcome

I'm Onyi, a digital storyteller and media curator with a background in Computer Science (BSc). I explore culture, identity, and introspection through short-form video, essays, and visual content.

My creative platform, Onyiverse, spans TikTok (12K+ followers) and Substack (2.7K+ subscribers), where I build reflective narratives that blend aesthetic storytelling with emotional honesty.

Whether through candid film photography, personal writing, or curated digital visuals, my goal is to create work that feels intentional, warm, and human.
